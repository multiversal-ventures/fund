Key Insight: The top 6 metro areas account for over 75% of all electricians in California. While Los Angeles has the most electricians by sheer count (21,070), Sacramento and Santa Rosa-Petaluma have the highest concentration relative to their workforce (location quotients of 1.14 and 1.04), meaning electricians make up a larger-than-average share of jobs there. San Jose offers the highest median pay at $95,020/year.
